Youssef's Journey

Education and Work Experience

2006 - 2010

I obtained my Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Information System from King Saud University (Riyadh, Saudi Arabia) with a GPA of 4.1 out of 5. My graduation project was about detecting phishing websites.

2011

I worked as a Teacher Assistant (TA) in the College of Computer Engineering and Sciences at Prince Sattam bin Abdulaziz University. While there, I was the TA for the Decisions Support System (IS4251) course.

2012-2016

I obtained my Master of Science (MSc) degree in Computer Science from the University of Alberta (Edmonton, Alberta, Canada) with a GPA of 3.85 out of 4. My research project was divided into two parts. The first is to compute the probability of connectivity of an underwater wireless sensor network (UWSN). The second is to compute the probability of detection of mobile RFID tags. The thesis title is "Object Mobility in Radio Frequency Identification Systems and Underwater Sensor Networks", which can be found here.

2016-2017

I worked as a Lecturer in the College of Computer Engineering and Sciences at Prince Sattam bin Abdulaziz University. While there, I taught the Computer Programming 1 (CS1301) and Database Management Systems (IS3511) courses.

2017-2022

I obtained my Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degree in Computer Science from Imperial College London (London, UK). My research project was about estimating the moisture content in agricultural products using RF signals (a.k.a. SING). The project focused on grapes as a proof-of-concept and involved developing signal processing techniques to cope with the geometrically challenging shape of grape clusters. The outcome of the project is a scientific paper and a Ph.D. thesis. The paper title is "SING: Free-Space SensING of Grape Moisture Using RF Shadowing" and it is published in IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement. The thesis title is "Moisture Estimation for Precision Agriculture through RF Sensing" and it is published in Imperial College London Spiral repository.

2022-Current

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the College of Computer Engineering and Sciences at Prince Sattam bin Abdulaziz University.
